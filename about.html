About Mealea

Welcome to MEALEA
- a fine dining Khmer restaurant that invites you to savor the rich history and exquisite flavors of Cambodia.
Established in 2005, Mealea has a long and cherished history of serving authentic Khmer cuisine.
Our dedication to preserving the culinary heritage of Cambodia is reflected in every dish we create.
